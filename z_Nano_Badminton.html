Something to Remember a Nanocrystal by...

In the original award-winning micrograph of nc-NiO an additional small cluster of atoms could be seen where the cartoon hat now sits (see ref. [D1]). The picture evoked a badminton racket and a shuttlecock rather than a human head. Hence the title quoted in the award certificate (Fig. a2). When a Chinese student (called Qiu-Qing) saw the original picture at our laboratory, she said it reminded her of a little boy's head and a bumblebee. The additional atomic cluster, however, proved to be a mere artifact and was afterwards eliminated. So, the bumblebee made way for the magic hat. This episode illustrates how a nanocrystal singled out from trillions upon trillions of others may serve as a source of varied inspiration.
